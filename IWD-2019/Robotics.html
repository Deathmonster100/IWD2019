ROBOTICS


  • I am part of the VEX Robotics team at the South Brunswick High School. I developed an interest in robotics and building stuff when I was still in elementary school. I thought that it was fun to innovate and create, basically making cool new toys and creations that were fun to use and play with. I went from using legos, to legos mindstorms, and eventually to VEX right now. After doing VEX Robotics for a year now, I developed a love for robotics because of how fun it is and, suprisingly, how competitive it is. Sure it is a bunch of nerds competing against each other using robots, but that doesn't mean it can't be fun, cool, and competitive.
